Prototype 2: Improved Team A ESP32-C3 Protective Case

Design Improvements

Prototype 2 builds on the previous design by removing the obstruction caused by the internal bar and improving overall usability.

  • Bar Removed: The additional bar causing lid interference was removed.
  • Better Structure: Improved internal layout for easier fitting of components.
  • Cleaner Design: Simplified shape for better usability and appearance.
  • Access Openings: Improved cutouts for ports and pins.
  • Compact Form: Maintains a small size while improving functionality.

Design Flow

Step 1: Review of Prototype 1
Identified that the internal bar caused the lid fitting issue.
Step 2: Design Modification
Removed the additional bar and simplified the case structure.
Step 3: 3D Modeling Update
Updated CAD design with new structure and layout adjustments.
Step 4: 3D Printing
Attempted to print the revised design.
Step 5: Issues Identified
The measurements were incorrect, leading to improper fitting. Additionally, difficulties were encountered during the 3D printing process, affecting the quality of the prototype.
Step 6: Future Improvements
Plan to refine measurements and optimize the design for better 3D printability and accuracy.
