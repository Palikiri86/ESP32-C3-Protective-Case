Improved ESP32-C3 Zero Protective Case

Overview

This design is an improved version of the earlier ESP32-C3 Zero protective case. The main goal of this revision is to enhance the safety of the exposed pins while maintaining a compact and functional structure.

Pin Protection Enhancement

One of the key improvements in this design is the added protection around the pins. The updated enclosure provides better shielding, reducing the risk of accidental damage, bending, or short circuits during handling and usage.

Improved Measurements

The measurements of the case have been refined to achieve a more precise fit for the ESP32-C3 Zero board. These adjustments ensure that the board sits securely inside the enclosure without unnecessary movement, improving overall stability and durability.

Design Refinements

The structure has been optimized for better alignment between components, making assembly easier and more reliable. The updated dimensions also improve the overall balance between protection and accessibility.

Conclusion

This improved design provides better protection, especially for sensitive pins, while offering a more accurate and reliable fit for the ESP32-C3 Zero. It is well suited for both prototyping and real-world applications.
