Final Design: ESP32-C3 Protective Case

The final design successfully improves on previous prototypes by using accurate measurements, improving usability, and adding proper ventilation. This results in a reliable, durable, and fully functional protective case for the ESP32-C3.

  • Perfect Fit: All dimensions were corrected, ensuring the lid fits securely.
  • Improved Usability: Easy access to USB ports, buttons, and GPIO pins.
  • Ventilation: Ventilation slots were added to reduce heat buildup.
  • Durability: Strong structure suitable for long-term use.
  • 3D Print Optimized: Uses minimal material while remaining cost-effective.

Testing Results

The final ESP32-C3 protective case was tested to ensure it met all project requirements. Testing focused on fit, accessibility, ventilation, durability, and print quality.

  • Fit Test: The ESP32-C3 board fitted securely inside the case with no unwanted movement.
  • Accessibility Test: USB ports, buttons, and GPIO pins remained accessible.
  • Ventilation Test: Ventilation slots allowed airflow and helped minimize heat buildup.
  • Durability Test: The case maintained its shape and strength during normal handling.
  • 3D Printing Test: The design printed successfully with minimal support material and no major defects.

All tests were completed successfully, confirming that the final design met the project requirements.

Conclusion

Throughout the design process, multiple prototypes were developed, tested, and evaluated to improve the overall quality of the ESP32-C3 protective case. Feedback from earlier prototypes was used to refine the dimensions, improve accessibility, and introduce effective ventilation.

The final design provides a secure fit for the ESP32-C3 while allowing easy access to all essential components. It is durable, practical, and optimized for efficient 3D printing.

Overall, the project successfully demonstrates the engineering design process by identifying problems, creating solutions, testing prototypes, and producing a final design that meets the intended requirements.
