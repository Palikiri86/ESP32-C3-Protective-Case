Final Design: ESP32-C3 Protective Case

The final design successfully improves on previous prototypes by using accurate measurements, improving usability, and adding proper ventilation. This results in a reliable, durable, and fully functional protective case for the ESP32-C3.

  • Perfect Fit: All dimensions were corrected, ensuring the lid fits securely.
  • Improved Usability: Easy access to ports, buttons, and GPIO pins.
  • Ventilation: Ventilation slots reduce heat buildup.
  • Durability: Strong structure suitable for long-term use.
  • 3D Print Optimized: Uses minimal material and is cost-effective.

Testing Results

The final ESP32-C3 protective case was tested to ensure it met all design requirements.

  • Fit Test: ESP32-C3 board fitted securely without movement.
  • Accessibility Test: USB port, buttons and GPIO pins remained accessible.
  • Ventilation Test: Airflow through ventilation slots helped reduce heat buildup.
  • Durability Test: Case maintained strength during handling and normal use.
  • 3D Printing Test: Printed successfully with minimal support material.

All tests were successfully completed and the final design met the project requirements.

Conclusion

Through multiple design iterations and testing, a reliable ESP32-C3 protective case was successfully developed. Feedback from Prototype 1 and Prototype 2 helped identify problems related to fit, ventilation, and usability.

The final design provides strong protection while maintaining access to ports, buttons, and GPIO connections. It is durable, cost-effective, and optimized for 3D printing.

Overall, the project demonstrates the successful application of the design process to create a practical housing solution for the ESP32-C3 microcontroller.
