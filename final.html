Testing Results

The final ESP32-C3 protective case was tested to ensure it met all design requirements. Several tests were performed to evaluate fit, accessibility, ventilation, and durability.

  • Fit Test: The ESP32-C3 board fit securely inside the case without movement.
  • Port Accessibility Test: USB port, buttons, and GPIO pins remained easily accessible.
  • Ventilation Test: Vent openings allowed airflow and reduced heat buildup during operation.
  • Durability Test: The case maintained its shape and strength during normal handling.
  • 3D Printing Test: The design printed successfully with minimal support material and no major defects.

All tests were successfully passed, demonstrating that the final design meets the project's functional and performance requirements.

Conclusion

Through the design, prototyping, and testing process, a functional and reliable protective case for the ESP32-C3 was successfully developed. Feedback gained from Prototype 1 and Prototype 2 was used to refine the dimensions, improve accessibility, and add ventilation features.

The final design provides excellent protection for the ESP32-C3 while maintaining easy access to all important components. The case is durable, cost-effective, and optimized for efficient 3D printing. Testing confirmed that the design satisfies the project's requirements and is suitable for long-term use.

Overall, the project demonstrates the successful application of the engineering design process to create an effective solution for protecting and housing an ESP32-C3 microcontroller.
