Phase 3: Final Design and Testing

Overview

Phase 3 represents the final stage of the project, where the improved design is completed, tested, and prepared for practical use. This phase focuses on refining all components and ensuring the design meets the required performance and safety standards.

Design Improvements

In this phase, the case design incorporates all previous improvements, including enhanced pin protection and more accurate measurements. The enclosure is adjusted to ensure a secure fit for the ESP32-C3 Zero board while maintaining accessibility.

Testing and Validation

The design is tested to confirm that the board fits correctly and that the case provides adequate protection. Special attention is given to the alignment of the pins, ensuring they are not exposed to damage during handling.

Final Adjustments

Any minor issues identified during testing are corrected. This may include small changes to dimensions, improving tolerances, or refining structural elements for better durability.

Final Outcome

The completed design is a reliable, well-fitted protective case for the ESP32-C3 Zero. It offers improved safety, better usability, and a clean overall appearance, making it suitable for both prototyping and real-world applications.
