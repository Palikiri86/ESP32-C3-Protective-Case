Prototype 1: Team A ESP32-C3 Protective Case

Design Details

This prototype focuses on creating a durable and functional protective case for the ESP32-C3 development board.

  • Material: PLA or ABS plastic for lightweight durability.
  • Ventilation: Built-in vents to reduce overheating.
  • Access Points: Cutouts for USB, pins, and buttons.
  • Mounting: Screw holes for secure installation.
  • Compact Size: Snug fit around the board.
  • Signal Friendly: No interference with Wi-Fi/Bluetooth.

Design Flow

Step 1: Requirement Analysis
Identified need for protection, access, and compact design.
Step 2: Initial Sketch
Created rough layout including lid and base structure.
Step 3: 3D Modeling
Designed the case and lid using CAD software.
Step 4: Prototype Fabrication
3D printed the case using PLA material.
Step 5: Testing Issue Identified
The lid did not fit properly onto the case due to the inclusion of an additional bar in the design, which caused interference and prevented proper closure.
Step 6: Design Improvement
Adjusted measurements and tolerances to ensure proper lid fitting.
